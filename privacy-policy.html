Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  ·  Version 1.0

The short version: DD-420 stores everything on your device. We collect no personal data. Nothing is sent to any server. No accounts. No tracking. No ads. Your information stays yours — always.

1. Who We Are

DD-420 is an independent iOS wellness application designed to help military veterans track PTSD symptoms and cannabis use. The app was built by a US Army combat medic veteran, for veterans — from personal experience with PTSD and the tools that help manage it. It is published as a sole proprietorship.

Questions about this policy: mtcombatmedic@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data.

DD-420 does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any identifying information.

All data you enter — check-ins, symptom logs, cannabis sessions, notes, settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Health or medical records
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Behavioral tracking or session data
  • Financial information

4. On-Device Storage Only

Every piece of information you log — symptoms, cannabis sessions, moods, triggers, notes — lives entirely on your iPhone. We have no servers that receive your data. There is no cloud sync. There is no backend database.

When you delete the app, all of your data is deleted with it.

5. PDF Export and Sharing

DD-420 lets you generate a PDF wellness report to share with your VA provider or therapist. This PDF is generated entirely on your device. It is not sent to any server. You control where it goes — you initiate the share, and iOS handles delivery. We never see the contents of any report you generate.

6. Apple Health (HealthKit)

DD-420 may request access to Apple Health data (such as sleep) to enrich your insights. If you grant access, that data is read locally on your device and used only within the app. No HealthKit data is transmitted off your device by DD-420.

7. In-App Purchases (DD-420 Plus)

DD-420 offers an optional paid subscription called DD-420 Plus. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. We receive only a confirmation from Apple that a subscription is active — no financial details.

8. Notifications

DD-420 may request permission to send local notifications (such as daily check-in reminders). These are generated and delivered entirely on your device by iOS. We do not use push notification services. No data leaves your device as part of the notification system.

9. Children's Privacy

DD-420 is designed for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used this app, contact us and we will assist in removing any locally stored data.

10. Third-Party Services

DD-420 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google, Firebase, Mixpanel, or similar platforms in the app.

The only third-party platform involved is Apple — through the App Store, HealthKit, and StoreKit — governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

11. Security

Because your data never leaves your device, security is tied to your iPhone's security. We recommend using a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. If your device is lost or stolen, your data is protected by iOS device encryption.

12. Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our end. All of your data is on your device, under your control. You can delete it at any time by deleting the app or clearing data in the app's settings.

13. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here and update the effective date. If the changes are material, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of DD-420 after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
